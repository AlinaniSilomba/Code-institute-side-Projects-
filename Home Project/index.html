How To Make Fruit Infused Tea

An exquisite tea blend combining berries, blueberries, ginger, orange, mint leaves with chamomile tea

Ingredients

  • 2 chamomile tea bags
  • 1 orange
  • 50 gms berries
  • 50 gms blueberries
  • 10 gms ginger
  • 8-10 mint leaves
  • 2 cups boiling water

Preparation

  1. Brew together camomile tea bags, orange rind, berries, blueberries, mint leaves & ginger for four minutes
  2. Serve hot enjoy!
